Fleet Standards

All Ghost Unit vehicles must follow approved livery, equipment, and usage protocols. Keep fleet presentation professional and avoid unauthorized modifications.

High Command

Date of Issue: 10/16/25
Last updated: 10/16/25
Approved by: GU-001 | saabsterz

Authorized vehicles

  • Mobile Command 2005
  • Chevlon Corbeta RZR 2014

Customization preset (mandatory)

  • Main light options: Visor Lights, Legacy Lightbar
  • Rear light options: Rear Window Lights
  • Additional lighting: Side Window Lights, Plate Lights, Siderunner
  • Accessories: Pushbar (Optional), Wraparound Bar, ALPR (Optional)
  • Texture: FHP Ghost Unit | WL | White, FHP Ghost Unit | WL
  • Antennas (Sedan): 5G Antenna (Trunk Right), Long Range Antenna (Rear Center), Low Profile Antenna (Front Center)
  • Antennas (SUV): 5G Antenna (Right), Long Range Antenna (Rear/Center), Low Profile Antenna (Front)
  • Spotlights: LED Spotlight (Optional), Passenger Spotlight (Optional)

If a customization option is not listed, you may not use it.

Leaking this document will lead to your immediate termination.

Special Response Team (SRT)

Date of Issue: 10/16/25
Last updated: 12/12/25
Approved by: GU-001 | saabsterz

Vehicle roster

  • 2008 Chevlon Camion PPV - Probationary Operative+
  • 2015 Bullhorn Prancer Pursuit - Probationary Operative+
  • Equipment Trailer - Probationary Operative+
  • 2019 Chevlon Plotoro - Operative+
  • 2020 Emergency Services Falcon Advance+ - Senior Operative+
  • 2011 SWAT Truck - Senior Operative+ when there are more than 4 available S.R.T. units and with permissions of Chief+

Vehicle guidelines

  • Lightbar: Legacy Lightbar, Visor Lights
  • Additional lighting: Side Window Lights, Plate Lights, Front Bumper Lights (ES Falcon Advance+), Fender Lights, Fog Lights, Small Siderunners, Siderunners (SO+), Rear Light Stick
  • Accessories: Trailer Hitch, LED Spotlights, Flood Lights (ES Falcon Advance+), ALPR (Optional)
  • Antennas: 5G Antenna - Right (Front Right on ES Falcon Advance+), Low Profile Antenna - Front Center, Long Range Antenna - Rear Center/Center (Rear on ES Falcon Advance+), Long Range Antenna (rear Center/ trunk center), Low Profile Antenna (Front Center)
  • Spotlights: LED Spotlight, Passenger Spotlight (Optional)

If a customization option is not listed, you may not use it. Additional customizations are listed in the SRT handbook.

Leaking this document will lead to your immediate termination.
