Who we are

The Ghost Unit is one of the most prestigious departments within FSRP. From everyday encounters to special operations and high-speed pursuits, our troopers exemplify excellence in all facets of law enforcement. Our commitment to professionalism and community safety sets us apart.

Reduce Speed—Arrive Alive

Within FHP, every trooper carries the responsibility of life itself. From routine patrols to high-risk responses, our commitment to vigilance, precision, and professionalism defines who we are. Safety is not just a rule, it is a promise we uphold in every decision, every action, and every moment on the road. Our dedication to protecting the community is unrivaled, and excellence guides every step we take.

Our Sub-Divisions

HSPU SRT

The Ghost Unit is comprised of two specialized subdivisions, each with its own unique focus and operational fields. Whether it's high-speed pursuits or tactical response scenarios, our subdivisions are equipped to handle a variety of law enforcement challenges with expertise.

  • High Speed Pursuit Unit — HSPU
  • Special Response Team — SRT
